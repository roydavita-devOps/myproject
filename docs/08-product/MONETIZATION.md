# Monetization

## Basic Plan

Target:

- Early-stage local businesses.
- Owners who need a simple public website.

Includes:

- Standard Templates.
- Basic website publishing.
- Essential business profile.
- WhatsApp and contact CTAs.

## Pro Plan

Target:

- Businesses that need stronger presentation and conversion.

Includes:

- Premium Templates.
- Better visual layouts.
- More advanced sections.
- Higher perceived brand quality.

## Premium Plan

Target:

- Businesses that want high-end positioning.
- Businesses in competitive industries.

Includes:

- Luxury Templates.
- Exclusive Industry Templates.
- Premium template packs.
- Future advanced publishing or customization features.

## Future Revenue Streams

- Subscription.
- Template Marketplace.
- Premium Template Packs.
- Industry Template Packs.
- Assisted setup services.
- Add-on visual assets.

## Monetization Principle

The product should stay simple for small business owners while still providing a clear upgrade path:

```text
Standard website -> Better template -> Premium brand presentation
```
