# Product Vision

## SaaS Vision

UMKM Builder is a SaaS platform for small local businesses that need a fast, reliable, and visually credible public website without hiring a developer.

The product should help business owners:

- Create a professional online presence quickly.
- Publish a website with contact, location, menu/services, gallery, and social proof.
- Share the website through WhatsApp and other common channels.
- Improve customer trust with better design and clearer business information.

## Template Marketplace Vision

UMKM Builder should evolve from a functional website generator into a template marketplace for local businesses.

The marketplace should provide:

- Standard templates for basic websites.
- Premium templates for more polished commercial presentation.
- Luxury templates for high-end positioning.
- Industry-specific templates for common local business categories.

## Product Principle

Business Type is not the same as Template.

- Business Type = recommendation and onboarding signal.
- Template = user-selectable website design.

This keeps the product flexible and supports future marketplace monetization.

## Monetization Strategy

Initial monetization should be subscription based.

Potential plan structure:

| Plan | Positioning | Template Access |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Basic | Entry plan for simple local websites. | Standard templates. |
| Pro | Better visual quality and business presentation. | Standard and Premium templates. |
| Premium | High-end presentation for competitive businesses. | Standard, Premium, Luxury, and Exclusive templates. |

Additional revenue streams:

- Premium template packs.
- Industry template packs.
- Marketplace add-ons.
- Assisted setup services.

## Premium Template Roadmap

Premium templates should focus on business outcomes:

- Better trust.
- Better visual hierarchy.
- Better mobile conversion.
- Stronger WhatsApp/contact CTAs.
- Better product/service presentation.

Near-term premium template categories:

- Restaurant Luxury.
- Cafe Modern.
- Clinic Professional.
- Laundry Clean Service.
- Corporate Executive.

## Long-Term Direction

UMKM Builder should become a practical website operating system for local businesses:

- Website builder.
- Template marketplace.
- Contact and lead capture.
- Business profile management.
- Publishing and sharing workflow.
- Subscription and premium asset ecosystem.
